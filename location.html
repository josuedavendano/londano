How do I get there?

By Train:

Take the 6 train to 23rd Street and walk eastward to Lexington Avenue. Walk north to 25th street. We're on the right!

By Car:

If you're coming from the Upper East Side, please take the FDR Drive south and take exit 8. Take East 37th Street to Lexington Avenue. Turn left on Lexington Avenue, and go to 25th Street.

If you're coming from the Queens, follow I-495 W and the Queens Midtown Tunnel and it will take you to East 35th Street in Manhattan. Turn right on East 35th Street and turn left on Lexington Avenue. Drive down to 25th Street and we're on the left!
